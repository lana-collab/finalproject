About Spikeball

Spikeball is a fast-paced net game played with a small trampoline-like net and a ball. It's super fun at the beach, park, or backyard! You'll get a great workout, better reflexes, and a lot of laughs with friends.

Equipment You Need

  1. Basic Set
    • Net (circular frame + legs)
    • Small rubber ball
  2. Extras
    • Extra balls
    • Knee pads or athletic shoes
  3. Playing Area
    • Grass lawn, beach, or backyard
    • Sufficient open space for 3-4 players
